Hostinger is Recommended by Neil Patel as the Best Web Hosting Provider in 2021

Neil Patel is a New York Times Bestselling author, entrepreneur, and top web influencer. Forbes notices him as one of the top 10 marketers, President Obama recognizes him as a top 100 entrepreneur under the age of 30, and Entrepreneur Magazine lists him as a creator of one of the 100 most brilliant companies. By helping businesses with innovative strategies, Neil Patel has gained immense popularity with his entrepreneurial and digital marketing skills. 

We are delighted to be announced as Neil Patel’s top pick for best overall web hosting services, VPS hosting plans, and WordPress hosting. This article shares the significant reasons behind it. 

#1 Web Hosting Services

Having a reliable web host is a must in today’s web world. Therefore, to find the best web hosting provider, Neil Patel focuses on the quality of services and checks uptime reliability, loading speed, and customer service excellence. 

He shares recommendations for the best web hosting services in 2021 and lists Hostinger as the first choice for those customers searching specifically for reliable shared hosting services. Affordable prices, instant loading speed, and daily website backups are noticed as the major contributing factors. Also, the expert 24/7/365 Hostinger customer support is mentioned as a massive perk for enterprises with a small or non-existent IT staff. 

#1 WordPress Hosting

Neil Patel has been using WordPress blogs as a principal part of his business for many years. Thus substantial hands-on experience allows evaluating multiple web hosting options. Shared vs. Managed WordPress hosting opportunities, traffic volume, speed, uptime, and technical support are considered to compare offerings. 

Hostinger is mentioned as the best WordPress website host. Rock-bottom pricing, an essential package of features and resources necessary to create, design, and manage a WordPress site, and the ease of use even for the first-time creator are the key characteristics leading to success here. The supporting factors include the free email account, domain name, CDN, and a free SSL certificate and ensure a smooth, fast and secure online journey. 

Hostinger has optimized the WordPress experience for customers – the LiteSpeed cache accelerates sites performance and works nicely with most popular plugins. Additionally, a helpful set of tools to get the site looking professional is offered to take advantage of and improve it at no cost.

#1 VPS Hosting Plans

VPS allows customers to get a private slice of server resources; thus, getting one of the VPS hosting plans could drastically improve site performance. Neil Patel emphasizes the capacity of resources not to worry, distinction of total control vs. shared responsibility, timely and tech-savvy support as the primary reasons for his choice.   

According to him, the best VPS hosting services for most people are offered by Hostinger. Attractive prices and an approachable interface make this service exceptionally splendid for first-time users. Even if it is a semi-managed plan offering a great mix of support and freedom to scale, Hostinger customer support agents are available, and additional helpful content is always linked. Also, a solid range of OS choices, including CentOS, Ubuntu, Fedora, Debian, or Suse, are included and need only one click to install.
