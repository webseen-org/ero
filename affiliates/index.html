Are you an affiliate marketer? We created the Namecheap Affiliate Program so you can earn money for helping us spread the word. Why Become a Namecheap Affiliate?

High conversion

Since 2000, Namecheap has been building a reputation as the web's finest provider of domain registration, SSL and web hosting services. Not to mention our top-notch product support and guarantee. Our stellar reputation means high conversion rates for our affiliates.

Quick & easy setup

It takes only a few minutes to place a Namecheap affiliate link on your site and start generating commissions. Our banners and text links provide compelling referral offers, and our support team is always just a few clicks away to answer your questions. So sign up today!

Regular payouts

As an affiliate, you earn commission every time a new customer makes their first purchase with Namecheap after clicking your link. And we make sure it’s no hassle to collect your commission. Our internal program offers multiple payout options.

How It Works

Access your affiliate account

Sign up for one of affiliate platforms listed below. After your account is approved you will get access to affiliate text links and banners with your affiliate ID applied.

Share your affiliate link

Use your affiliate ID to refer to any Namecheap page or choose one of our banners. Display the link or banner on your site, social media or emails.

Watch your commissions grow

You earn actual dollars for every new customer you refer to us. We provide real-time reporting on your earnings and regular payouts.
