WHOIS Privacy Service Agreement

  1. INTRODUCTION

    Namecheap, as a proud leader in online privacy, provides free domain registration privacy service for all eligible domains. The domain registration privacy service is offered using a third-party service provider selected by Namecheap (“WHOIS Privacy Service”).

    This WHOIS Privacy Service Agreement (“WHOIS PSA”) sets forth the terms that apply when you use, whether by purchase or free, WHOIS Privacy Service provided by Namecheap. This WHOIS PSA supplements and is in addition to any other Namecheap terms that apply to you and your use of Namecheap services, such as our Universal Terms of Service and Domain Registration Agreement. By using or continuing to use WHOIS Privacy Service offered by Namecheap, you acknowledge and agree to these terms.

    In this WHOIS PSA, “you” and “your” refer to you and any agent, employee, servant or person authorized to act on your behalf, and the Namecheap Registrant listed in the WHOIS contact information for the domain name. This WHOIS PSA explains Namecheap’s obligations to you in connection with your WHOIS Privacy Services and explains your obligations to Namecheap.

  2. PRIVACY SERVICES

    Nameheap has selected WithHeldForPrivacy (WFP) as its third-party provider of WHOIS Privacy Services. WFP, an Icelandic company subject to the GDPR, was chosen because of its unmatched privacy-by-design approach for the provision of privacy services for domain WHOIS information.

    WFP’s privacy services include the use of its contact information (address, phone and email) for all relevant WHOIS fields that may be otherwise publicly displayed. Accordingly, as part of this service, WFP may be contacted by third parties who wish to reach you as the domain registrant. WFP will provide a privacy protected means for such third parties to reach you.

    Your personal and WHOIS information will not be shared with WFP. WFP does not collect, nor are they provided with, your personal information.

    However, Namecheap’s provision of WFP’s privacy service for WHOIS Privacy Services is subject to your compliance with these terms.

    You may find more information about WFP by visiting their site. See https://withheldforprivacy.com/.

    The WHOIS Privacy Services offered by WFP provide the use of WFP’s physical address and phone number as a means for third parties to reach you. It also provides an anonymized email address for your use. With WFP’s services, Namecheap is provided with the technological solution to use such anonymized email as a forwarding mechanism to the email address you have on file with Namecheap. When third parties to try communicate with you via WFP’s physical address, phone or anonymized email, you agree to the following terms:

Thereafter, when messages are sent to the email address listed in the WFP Address, the message will be automatically forwarded to the email address you provided to Namecheap. If the email address you have with Namecheap becomes non-functioning or if the email to such address bounces, you agree that neither Namecheap nor WFP shall not be obligated to attempt to contact you through other means.
