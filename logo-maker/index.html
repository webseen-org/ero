Frequently asked questions

Where can I find my saved logos?

Accessing your saved logos is easy on Logo Maker. First, sign in to your Namecheap account and then click on the View my existing logos link at the top of the main Logo Maker page. From there you will be navigated to all of your saved logos.

How much is it after I make my logo?

Your logo is always free on Logo Maker. No matter if you download your logo once or thousands of times, it will always be free.

How do I download a logo?

Downloading a logo is simple and easy on Logo Maker. If you’re creating a logo from scratch, complete all the necessary design steps and then click on the download button at the end of the process. However, in order to download a logo, you will need to be signed in to your Namecheap account. If you do not have one, you can sign-up for a free account and download your logo.

If you’re looking to download a saved logo, sign into your Namecheap account and click on the View my existing logos link on the main Logo Maker page. From there, select the logo you’d like to download. After selection, click on the download button and then the files will automatically be downloaded to your computer.

Can I view my logo as it would appear on my business marketing material?

Yes, it's quick and simple to see how your new logo will look in all its glory on things like websites, t-shirts, and business cards. You can even share how it looks with others through online links.

Do I have to sign up to use Logo Maker?

You are totally free to play and create as many logos as you’d like. However, to download or share your free logos, you will need to sign into your Namecheap account. Logo Maker is free for everyone, so they can take their online businesses further.

Can I copyright a logo from Logo Maker?

In order to trademark a logo, you'd have to work with a lawyer familiar with trademark laws in your country. Unfortunately, we can't help you with this.

How do I create a business logo?

Easy! Just hit Create free logos and follow the simple steps to use our online logo maker. And then in a few short minutes, you’ll have a new logo to download and share.

Can I create a vector logo with Logo Maker?

Yes. For all of the designed logos on Logo Maker, you'll receive them in two logo file formats: SVG and PNG.

What size should a logo from Logo Maker be?

We give you a variety of logo sizes to try to best fit all of your needs: banner imagery, profile photos, etc.
