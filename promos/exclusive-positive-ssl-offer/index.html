About Comodo PositiveSSL Certificate

PositiveSSL is one of the most popular and inexpensive certificates in the industry. It's an ideal choice for websites where the brand's trust is already established and organization verification is not needed. It's also suitable for securing low-volume e-commerce websites.

Features at a glance

Online Validation

The purpose of online validation is to check if the person applying for a certificate has the right to request a certificate for the domain. There's no paperwork!

Free Site Seal

The PositiveSSL site seal is a small logo you can place anywhere on your website to provide immediate visual assurance that your site is genuine and authenticated.

Speedy Issuance

In most cases, the PositiveSSL certificate can be issued in just a few minutes. Be sure you have a dedicated IP address, a CSR and correct contact info in Whois.

$10,000 Warranty

This is the amount payable to your site visitors, if they ever incur loss from an online credit card transaction as a result of a mis-issued PositiveSSL certificate.

Unlimited Reissues

In case you've changed your contact details, changed your hosting company or lost the private key post-purchase, you can get your certificate replaced for FREE.

128/256 Bit Encryption

The encryption level refers to the number of bits used to generate encryption key. PositiveSSL supports up to 256-bit encryption.
